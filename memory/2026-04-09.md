
## 2026-04-09 - v35 DB Stabilization & UI Upgrades
*   **Supabase Data Isolation (v35)**: Replaced legacy `platform_data` JSON dependencies with direct Supabase RDBMS queries for `staff`, `invoices`, and `hotel_item_prices` to ensure high scalability.
*   **Staff UI**: Fixed employee registration bugs, lifted the "Lite Plan" 1-user restriction, and removed delete invoice functionality for staff to secure business operations. Guaranteed staff IDs and names correctly map into the invoice issuance record.
*   **Special/General Hotel Invoices**:
    *   Rebuilt rendering for 'Special Hotel' (2-column category) and 'General Hotel' (Matrix layout).
    *   Corrected the view behavior to hide VAT and display only the **Total Supply Price** within individual viewing windows.
    *   Included the 3-tier total (Supply Price | VAT | Total Sum) strictly inside the **Print/Send popup** and **Monthly Settlement** views for clarity.
    *   Enforced chronological/DB-creation sequence ordering for item lists instead of alphabetical or arbitrary grouping.
*   **Dashboard Adjustments**: Modified the admin 'Top 10' and 'Trend' elements so that **Fixed-rate (정액제)** hotels exclusively log their pre-agreed fixed amounts, ignoring individual delivery values, ensuring accurate monthly recurring revenue stats.
*   **Monthly Settlement Management (월정산 발송)**: Unified invoice history grouping into a monthly package model. Admins can revoke batches securely (canceling the `is_sent` true flag), propagating the deletion instantly to the hotel partner view.
*   **Partner App Sync**: Re-connected the partner dashboard to fetch the actual aggregated DB values and track the "Confirm Settlement" (`confirmed_months`) status dynamically with Supabase.
